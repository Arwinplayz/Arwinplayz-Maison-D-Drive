Our History

Our Origins

Founded in 1967, Maison Dé Drive began as a humble boutique showroom dedicated to luxury cars. Our founders shared a passion for engineering excellence and timeless design, aiming to create an exclusive space where enthusiasts could experience automotive artistry firsthand.

Growth & Innovation

By 2015, we expanded nationally, embracing cutting-edge technology and customizations. Our expert team started collaborating with prestigious manufacturers to offer personalized vehicles, blending tradition with innovation to exceed every client’s expectations.

Commitment to Excellence

Maison Dé Drive is proud to uphold the highest standards of service. From meticulous maintenance to bespoke concierge experiences, every detail reflects our dedication to luxury, performance and customer satisfaction.

Looking Ahead

As we approach our 70 year milestone, we’re focused on sustainability and innovation. We’re exploring electric and hybrid luxury vehicles to ensure that Maison Dé Drive continues to lead in style, comfort and responsibility well into the future.
